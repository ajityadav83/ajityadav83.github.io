Some points about India

  • World's largest democracy
  • Second-most populous country in the world
  • Seventh largest country in the world in terms of area
  • Got independence in 1947 and is a federation consisting of 29 states and 7 union territories
  • India is notable for its religious diversity, with Hinduism, Buddhism, Sikhism, Islam, Christianity, and Jainism among the nation's major religions
